Bookshelf

A new initiative to provide you with good books at low cost.The money gained is used to buy new books and for the education of poor children with ROOH

We want to create an environment of sharing and introduce a habit of reading in NIT Kurukshetra. Our mission is NIT Kurukshetra to read more.For this purpose we are providing book at a negligible rent. Moreover the money collected through rent is used for the payment of school fee of poor children.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Bookshelf??

It is a new initiative of sharing and reading books in NIT Kurukshetra.

How can I order for a book?

You can check the available books and if the book of your interest is available you can order it by clicking here.

How much will it cost?

It costs almost negligible. You will be charged Rs 20 for 10 days. You can increase the number of days with some extra charge. For details visit Our blog.

What are the terms and conditions?

You will be required to deposit a security money of Rs 100 which will be returned to you once you return the book back to us.

What will you do with the collected amount?

We share half of the profits to owners of books and remaining half is used to purchase new books and in education of poor children.

I have also many books. How can I help you?

Please give us details of books by clicking here.
